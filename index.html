Q: What is AmfreOS?

AmfreOS is a Debian-based OS with integrated SOC tools for endpoint monitoring, IDS, threat intelligence, and forensic analysis.

Q: Who is it for?

Home labs, schools, clinics, and small offices seeking secure computing environments.

Q: Key Security Features?

  • Automatic Wazuh agent enrollment
  • Network auto-discovery and clustering
  • Firewall (UFW) & intrusion prevention (Fail2Ban)
  • Malware scanning (ClamAV, rkhunter, chkrootkit)
  • Auto-healing and hardening scripts
