AmfreOS

Secure, Privacy-Focused, SOC-Ready Linux

AmfreOS is designed for schools, clinics, home labs, and developers. It comes with integrated security tools, self-healing scripts, Wazuh monitoring, and privacy enhancements.

About AmfreOS

AmfreOS provides a secure, stable, and privacy-conscious computing environment for daily use and learning. It includes a pre-integrated SOC stack, auto-enrollment for endpoints, and advanced self-healing tools.

Privacy & Security

Minimal telemetry, built-in firewall, antivirus, and intrusion detection.

Stable & Reliable

Based on Debian stable with long-term support.

Education & Labs

Perfect for schools, clinics, home labs, and small offices.

Developer-Friendly
